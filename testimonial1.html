Main characters in Jujutsu Kaisen




    • Yuji Itadori (Japanese: 虎杖悠仁, Hepburn: Itadori Yūji) is a fictional character and the main protagonist of the manga series Jujutsu Kaisen created by Gege Akutami.
    • Satoru Gojo (Japanese: 五条 悟, Hepburn: Gojō Satoru) He is the strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer in the world, even rivalling and surpassing the King of Curses Sukuna himself.
    • Ryomen Sukuna (Japanese: 呂蒙スクナ, Hepburn: Ryomen Sukuna) Sukuna was the most powerful cursed spirit to have ever lived, and as such was considered the undisputed "King of Curses."
